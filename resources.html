Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about using ZeroWaste

How do I know if donated food is safe to eat?

All food donors on our platform are verified businesses that follow food safety protocols. We also provide guidelines on how to assess food quality and safety when picking up donations.

What types of food can be donated?

Restaurants and food businesses can donate prepared foods, baked goods, fresh produce, and packaged items that are still within their safe consumption period.

Are there any costs involved?

ZeroWaste is completely free for both food recipients and donors. Our mission is to make food assistance accessible to everyone in the community.

How quickly should I pick up donated food?

For food safety, we recommend picking up donated food within 2 hours of posting. Our app will send notifications to ensure timely pickup.

Can I donate non-food items?

Currently, our platform focuses specifically on food donations. However, we're exploring options to expand to other essential items in the future.
